Well Stimulation Acidizing

lecture 13

    Here’s a summary of acid stimulation in oil wells based on various sources:
  1. Company Introduction & Course Overview: The session introduces Reservoir Solutions, a company offering technical studies, petroleum courses, and mentorship programs. Their upcoming course on static and dynamic reservoir modeling offers lifetime access to recorded sessions and includes practical datasets for application【02:41–14:52】.
  2. Instructor Introduction: The speaker for the webinar is Mr. Payman Danishfur, a stimulation engineer with expertise in petroleum engineering, particularly in acidizing and CO2 injection【17:23–17:50】.
  3. Acidizing Techniques: The focus of the webinar is on acidizing, a stimulation technique used to improve well productivity by reducing the skin factor (formation damage). Acidizing methods include matrix acidizing and fracture acidizing【21:37–24:02】【37:29–46:09】.
  4. Factors Influencing Acidizing: Key factors like injection rate, temperature, and the type of acid (e.g., HCL and HF) are discussed. The session explains the role of acid in removing formation damage and improving permeability【34:39–55:11】.
  5. Diversion & Additives in Acidizing: The webinar concludes with a discussion on diversion techniques (mechanical and chemical), and the importance of additives (e.g., mutual solvents and iron control agents) in enhancing the acidizing process【1:05:44–1:24:56】.
