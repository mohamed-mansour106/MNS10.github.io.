Introduction to Well Stimulation, Dr. Ahmed Algarhy, Lecture 04/04

lecture 5

  1. Introduction and Moderators (00:00 - 01:07)
    • Shahad Bhajit and Esmachendi introduced themselves as moderators. They welcomed attendees on behalf of Pio Petro Arab Oil & Gas Academy and SPE Egypt Section, emphasizing professional conduct and the upcoming final exam.
  2. Lecture Focus (02:28 - 03:50)
    • Dr. Ahmed Algarhy introduced the topic of acidizing and acid fracturing, providing a basic overview of these techniques, which would be expanded in future learning. He also addressed the free services offered for students by Pio Petro and clarified the difference between mini-fracture tests and leak-off tests.
  3. Acidizing vs. Acid Fracturing (12:39 - 20:40)
    • Dr. Algarhy explained the differences between acidizing (injecting acid at lower pressures) and acid fracturing (injecting at higher pressures to fracture the formation), including applications for both carbonate and sandstone formations. He detailed how acid types, such as HCl and acetic acid, affect these processes.
  4. Design and Optimization of Acidizing Jobs (28:32 -45:55)
    • The lecture covered the technical aspects of designing acidizing treatments, emphasizing the importance of factors like acid concentration, injection rate, and formation characteristics. Dr. Algarhy also discussed the use of diverters and how to ensure uniform acid distribution in various zones of a reservoir.
  5. Final Remarks and Q&A (49:08 - 1:29:33)
    • Dr. Algarhy concluded the lecture with a discussion on the use of different acids, their properties, and when to use them for specific types of formations (e.g., limestone, dolomite, sandstone). He answered questions about the practical application of acidizing in the field and promised to share the slides through Google Classroom.
