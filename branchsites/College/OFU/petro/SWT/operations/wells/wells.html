US-13 L/S (Flow back and flow test operation)

    Open well back flow on critical flow
    • record whcip and annulus a and b
    • inform Bridge one hour before start flaring
    • check wind speed and wind direction
    • if wind speed less than 5 knots flow against sealine pressure

  2. If well able to flow : flow test well against sealine pressure as follows:
    • @40/64" +ve choke: stabilize flow for 5 hours
    • @32/64" +ve choke: stabilize flow for 5 hours
    • @24/64" +ve choke: stabilize flow for 5 hours

  3. If well unable to flow, will perform N2 Lift thru 2" CT

  4. R/U slick line lubricator PCE and pressure test
    • RUN#1: SET 2.31" BAKER FB2 TEST TOOL AT 2.31" F-NIPPLE @10337'
    • PRESSURIZE TUBING TO EQUALIZE PREEAURE /200-500 PSI OVER BALANCE TO 3000 PSI (EXPECTED WHCIP AFTER SSD OPEN: 2800 PSI / BASED ON W/L HISTORY
    • RUN#2: RIH W/ 2.75" B-SELF RELEASING POSITIONING TOOL W/ ROLLER STEM (10') TO OPEN 2.75: OTIS XU- SSD @10215'
    • RUN#3: RETRIEVE 2.31" BAKER FB2 TEST TOOL AT 2.31" F-NIPPLE @10337'

  5. BACK FLOW commingled long string for clean up against sealine

  6. Flow test well conmingled against sealine as following:
    • @40/64" +ve choke: stabilize flow for 5 hours
    • @32/64" +ve choke: stabilize flow for 5 hours
    • @24/64" +ve choke: stabilize flow for 5 hours
BEFORE opening for another formation we will equalize the tubing pressure with the new formation pressure the 2.75" B-SELF RELEASING POSITIONING TOOL W/ ROLLER STEM for the slick line it will remove the f-nipple to open for another level of formation Arab D4L & D5 We will flow the that long string Arab D4L & D5 (two formations level) commingled مختلطين together for flow back
    THE LEVELS OF FORMATION IS:
  • LOWER (L)
  • MIDEUM (M)
  • UPPER (U)
