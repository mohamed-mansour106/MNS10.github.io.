2. PLT operation

  • After rig up on the well Delta, well testing, pumping,
  • slick line to retrieve DHSV
  • E-logging connected the PLT fake tool with the coil tubing
  • coil tubing RIH in a dummy Run to check that there is no any obstructions
  • there was a vibration on the injector head so well testing open the well through sealine bypassin the separator
  • once coil tubing reach the depth, they started to POOH
  • for the main run E-logging connected the real PLT tool to coil tubing
  • Coil tubing started to RIH again to reach the wanted depth
  • while coil tubing Running in the hole, well testing is flowing the well to depressurize the wellbore
  • once they reach the depth they start corelation
  • if the information is not equal the depth with the information that he get it from the drilling engineer he started to calibration the depth with the data he get about that depth
  • after calibration and corelation again
  • well testing started flow Test and collecting data
  • E-logging and coil tubing are standby untill Stabilization
  • once the well is Stabilized
  • E-logging will start collecting data
