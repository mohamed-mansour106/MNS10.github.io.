Q&A Summary

    Sure! Here's a Q&A style summary based on the video "IPM2 - Body Protection Techniques"⚡:
  1. Q1: What is the primary objective of this module?

    Answer: ✅ A: To increase awareness of injury risks in daily work and teach body protection techniques to prevent them.

  2. Q2: What are the key benefits of enhanced body protection techniques?

    Answer: ✅A:

    • Increased hazard awareness
    • Greater strength and control
    • Safer, smarter work with less strain
    • Contribution to a strong daily safety culture

  3. Q3: What does body control require in the workplace?

    Answer: ✅ A: Power, balance, and stability during movement.

  4. Q4: What are the core techniques covered in this module?

    Answer: ✅ A:

    • Line of strength
    • Side stepping
    • Safe working zone
    • Smart grip
    • Watch your head
    • Bracing and spinal alignment

  5. Q5: How does “line of strength” help prevent injury?

    Answer: ✅ A: By aligning your body’s movement and force direction to reduce strain and improve stability.

  6. Q6: What is meant by “safe working zone”?

    Answer: ✅ A: Adjusting your position and tools to keep your work within a green, ergonomic area close to your body.

  7. Q7: What is the "smart grip" technique?

    Answer: ✅ A: Using strength fingers (not precision fingers) to activate larger muscle groups (back, hips, legs) for better control and safety.

  8. Q8: Why is head and spinal alignment important?

    Answer: ✅ A: To maintain center of gravity, reduce spinal stress, and improve balance and movement efficiency.
