Horner Method

Plot pressure vs. Horner time to estimate formation pressure. Straight-line extrapolation gives p* for radial flow regimes.

MDH Method

Use Miller-Dyes-Hutchinson to reduce effect of superposition and stabilize pressure derivative interpretation.

Average Reservoir Pressure

P* from Horner extrapolation equals Pi only in infinite reservoirs. For bounded systems, MBH correction charts or Dietz shape factors are needed to convert P* to Pavg.
