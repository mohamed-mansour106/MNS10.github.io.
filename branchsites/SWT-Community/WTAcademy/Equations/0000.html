Solution GOR Estimation ($R_s$)

Estimating gas solubility using Standing and Vasquez & Beggs correlations.

Standing (1947)

$$R_s = \gamma_g \left[ \frac{P}{18 \cdot 10^{yg}} \right]^{1.204}$$

Vasquez & Beggs

$$R_s = C_1 \gamma_g P^{C_2} \exp\left[ \frac{C_3 API}{T_{abs}} \right]$$
