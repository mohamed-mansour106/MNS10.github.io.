Choke Size Conversion

$$ID_{inch} = \frac{\text{Choke Size}}{64}$$
$$ID_{mm} = ID_{inch} \times 25.4$$
Show Symbols Definition (تعريف الرموز)
Symbol Definition Standard Unit
$\text{Choke Size}$ Orifice diameter in 64ths of an inch $1/64"$
$ID_{inch}$ Inner Diameter in decimal inches $inches$
$ID_{mm}$ Inner Diameter in metric units $mm$
$25.4$ Standard conversion factor (Inch to MM) $mm/inch$

* Logic: $1 \text{ inch} = 64/64$ and $1 \text{ inch} = 25.4 \text{ mm}$
