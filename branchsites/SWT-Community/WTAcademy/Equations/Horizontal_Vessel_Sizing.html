Vessel Design Concepts

The design relies on balancing two primary physical forces:

  • Liquid Residence:Providing sufficient vessel length for the liquid to remain calm, allowing gas bubbles to break free and escape to the surface.
  • Gas Velocity:Ensuring the gas speed in the upper section of the vessel is not high enough to "entrain" or carry oil droplets downstream (preventing carry-over).

Note:The K-factor used (0.35) is the industry standard value for horizontal separators equipped with a Mist Extractor.
