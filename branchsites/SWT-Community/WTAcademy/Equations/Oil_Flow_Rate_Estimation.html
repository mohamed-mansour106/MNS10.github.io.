Oil Flow Rate Estimation (Fixed Choke)

Estimation using Gilbert and Ros multi-phase flow equations.

Gilbert Equation

$$Q_o = \frac{P \times C^{1.89}}{435 \times (\frac{G}{1000})^{0.546}}$$

Ros Equation

$$Q_o = \frac{(P + 14.73) \times C^2}{17.4 \times G^{0.5}}$$
