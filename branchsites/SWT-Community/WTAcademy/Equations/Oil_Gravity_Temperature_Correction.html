Oil Gravity Temperature Correction

Convert observed Specific Gravity and API to standard conditions (60°F).

S.G @ 60°F

$$SG_{60} = SG_{obs} + \Delta SG_t$$

API @ 60°F

$$API_{60} = \frac{141.5}{SG_{60}} - 131.5$$
