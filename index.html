02 — Experience

Professional experience built in the field and behind the screen.

Current Role

December 2022 - Present

Well Testing Operator

Almansoori Petroleum Service (MPS), Abu Dhabi, UAE

  • Operated and controlled surface well testing operations to evaluate reservoir performance and fluid characteristics, including flowback, flaring, acid stimulation support, unloading, re-injection, cementing, milling, well kick-off, pigging, de-oiling, well clean-up, and well killing operations.
  • Performed oil and gas sampling and analysis, including BS&W, API gravity, water specific gravity, gas specific gravity (Ranarex), oil shrinkage, salinity (PTB), and H₂S & CO₂ detection using Dräger tubes.
  • Interpreted field data and charts (e.g., Barton charts) to support inflow, integrity, injectivity, and pressure testing operations.
  • Utilized data acquisition and monitoring systems such as Fekete and Modbus to track well performance.
  • Prepared detailed operational reports and managed Permit to Work (PTW) documentation in compliance with safety standards.
  • Executed critical field operations including burner boom positioning, well opening/closing, manifold maintenance and rebuilding, equipment greasing, and rigging setup/teardown while maintaining strict safety and operational accuracy.
  • Collaborated across multiple offshore and onshore well testing projects, working on rigs such as Al-Hail, Alreem, Al Saadiyat, Mehzem, and Makasib, as well as various Barges and vessels including Supporter, Surprise 2, Swissco, Azzam, Al Saqr, and INW3.
  • Gained experience across oil, gas, and water wells in diverse operational environments.
  • Operated and monitored key well testing equipment including separators, pumps, tanks, burner systems, choke manifolds, and control valves to ensure efficient and safe operations.
  • Utilized strong observational and mathematical skills while well testing Operations to ensure accurate data and safe, efficient operations.
  • Supported knowledge sharing and on-site guidance for junior crew members by explaining well testing processes, equipment functions, and data interpretation in a simplified and practical manner.
